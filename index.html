Cal Saw is the complete saw and knife technology package. The ultimate goal of providing the highest achievable cutting efficiency, longevity, and overall quality is the backbone of our custom designed and manufactured saws and knives. We strive to help you get more out of your precision cutting tools.

Our Philosophy

Since 1886, Cal Saw’s promise to customers has been to research, develop, and deliver the most advanced technology for a diverse range of cutting needs. Throughout the four generations of family stewardship, and now the collaboration with Roseburg Saw & Tool under the new ownership of Dan Lines, we’ve remained firmly rooted in the science and engineering of saws, knives, wood machining and food processing in order to keep that promise and help your business succeed.

No matter what your focus – whether you’re a filer seeking blades with lower maintenance, a QC specialist needing a higher quality product, or a corporate executive aiming for the right mix of value and results – we remain committed to your success. We’ll continue to custom design and manufacture advanced cutting solutions that turn your particular challenges into revenue-building and business-prospering results.
