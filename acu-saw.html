Acu Saw Centering Adaptor

The “Acu Saw” is a self-adjusting centering adaptor for spline arbor saws, intended for use in saw grinders and other circular saw maintenance equipment. Its engineering allows for much improved accuracy and is easy to use and maintain.

Features

  • Adaptor can be configured to accommodate lobe (Retec) or involute style splines
  • A simple snap on is all that’s required to mount a saw on an Acu Saw
  • Pre-loaded, hardened pins engage the spline of the saw to ensure the saw is clamped at an accurate center
  • The Acu Saw compensates for imperfections in the spline such as inaccurately indexed lobes, oval eyes, or uneven wear
  • The movable parts in the adaptor are separated sufficiently so as to allow grinding dust to be flushed away without affecting the mechanism

Specifications

  • Body is composed of anodized aluminum alloy
  • Pins are precision machined, hardened steel
  • Standard mounting bushing bore sizes are 5/8”, 3/4”, 7/8”, and 24mm
  • Adaptors come in four different sizes, with two possible pin arrangements for each size
